Cake Recipie:

By-Aditya

Sr.no Name of ingridients Quantity
1. Flour 500g
2. Egg 4
3. Vanila extract 50ml
4. Choclate syrup 500ml
5. Baking powder 50g

Procedure:

  1. Make batter by mixing eggs and flour.
  2. Put some vanilla extract for essence.
  3. Add choclate syrup for flavour.
  4. Add baking soda for making ka cake spongy.
  5. Now put the mixture in oven.
  6. Wait for 30 min and enjoy the cake.
