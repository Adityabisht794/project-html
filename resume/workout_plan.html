Workout split for optimal gains:

Workout split plays a major role in accomplishing an individuals physical goals.
There are two major workout split:
1.Bro split.
2.Push pull leg split.

Bro split:

Bro split is one of the most optimal split for beginers .
It consist of training a specific muscle group per day.
It is optimal for gaining muscle mass and bulking.
It also helps in progress of strengh devlopement.

Push pull leg split:

Push pull leg workout is optimal split for intermidiate lifters.
It consist of training two or more muscle group per day.
It is optimal for gaining muscle mass and hypertrophy.
It also helps in building endurance and strength of muscles.



Note:

To choose optimal split one should be aware of his body type.
